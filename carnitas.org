#+TITLE: Carnitas
* Ingredients
- Pork loin / pork shoulder
- Chicken broth
- Spices
  - Cinnamon
  - Oregano
  - Red pepper flakes
  - Salt
* Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients in slow cooker
2. Cook on high for 5 hours
3. Remove meat from slow cooker, shred
4. Cover meat with remaining broth from slow cooker to retain moisture

#+TITLE: Bean Bacon Soup
* Ingredients
- Bacon
- Ham hock
- White beans
- Onions
- Garlic
- Carrots
- Tomato paste
- Spices
  - Salt
  - Pepper
  - Thyme
  - Parsley
- Water / broth
* Directions
** Prep
- Soak beans
- Cook bacon, dice
- Sautee onions and carrots in grease from bacon
** Game time
1. Add all ingredients to slow cooker
2. Cook on low for 10 hours
3. Remove ham hock
